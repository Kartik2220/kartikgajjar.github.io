GTU PAPERS

GTU Previous Year Question Papers – Gujarat Technological University uploads the GTU Question Papers online at the completion of the university semester examinations. The University conducts the semester exam twice a year in Winter and Summer session. Gujarat Technological University previous year question papers are available in the pdf format. Here we provide the access to last three years GTU Question Papers. Check the page for GTU Previous Year Question Papers.First rule of GTU. Never use reference book. If u want to score. Use Techmax only strictly. For knowledge use reference book but i am sure you will not score much :p. Take previous year question paper(till last semester) and tick each and every question in your techmax(if not given). Then tick the questions asked in mid semester exams(other than gtu). Then comes the time of GTU exams. Never leave a single question, agar kuch na aata ho toh question ke bare m likho.

So basic tips are:
1- good , neat and readable handwriting… this is the most important thing which will help you get marks more than you expect.
2- draw neat and clean diagram with labels.. you can spend extra time on diagram than writing about that topic.. trust me , its worth it.
3- try to answer every question.. even though you dont know about it..try to write something related to it , maybe from that chapter. Your marks wont be deducted and in most cases you will get atleast 1–2 marks…
4- dont try to fill pages and write irrelevant thing. This will have two disadvantages -
(i) examiner will think you are stupid.
(ii) it will waste your time
